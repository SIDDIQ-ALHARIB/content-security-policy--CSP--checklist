## OWASP Top 10 Mapping

### A03:2021 – Injection
- Weak or missing CSP increases the impact of Cross-Site Scripting (XSS).

### A05:2021 – Security Misconfiguration
- Missing CSP or overly permissive directives.
- Missing security headers.

### A01:2021 – Broken Access Control
- Missing frame-ancestors can allow clickjacking attacks.

### A02:2021 – Cryptographic Failures
- Missing upgrade-insecure-requests or HSTS may allow mixed content.
